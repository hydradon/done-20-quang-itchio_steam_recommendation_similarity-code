Studio Thumpy Puppy is a game audio studio based in California.

Lindar K. Greenwood (they/them) is a multi-instrumentalist and composer of 15 years, lead composer for the studio, and has spent more time than anyone ever should playing with the Sonic the Hedgehog 2 soundtest menu.

Sandra Baker (she/her) is a hands-on, self-taught mix/recording engineer, co-composer, voice actor, amateur baking enthusiast, and Very Tall Girl.
