Timothi Ellim [born Timothi Lim] is a fiction author, game developer, and a visual artist who actively seeks to bring ideas to life through a variety of mediums. When asked what mythological creature he identifies with most, he would often say "Chimera".

Is always down to explore a museum or grab a cup of coffee.

Beyond personal projects, Timothi also develops creative process solutions to help individuals and companies unlock or improve their creative potential.

Singapore | United States | Currently studies Interactive Media at USC in LA
