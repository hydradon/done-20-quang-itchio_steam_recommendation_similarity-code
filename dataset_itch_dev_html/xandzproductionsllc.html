     X and Z Productions LLC. was founded by Xavier and Zygy in 2011, and has 7 games to its credit. Now is the time to go back to our roots and recapture the fire of our first game, Armies of Alamar. Such grand ideas we had back then, man oh man could we tell you stories… We just didn’t have the money or the following to make it a reality. So we had to side track it for a while and make ends meat. I am sure a lot of you know that feeling. But Alamar kept calling. 

     This time we are answering her call.

     We aren’t rich by any stretch of the imagination. But we are dedicated and when we say we are going to do something, we do it. We will always be upfront and honest with you, that’s our promise to all patrons: past, present and future.  At the end of the day, your belief in us is all that matters. That's what puts food on our table and gives us the energy each and every day to make this dream a reality.
