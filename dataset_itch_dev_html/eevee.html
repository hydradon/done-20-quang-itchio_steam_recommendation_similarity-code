Here are some video games.  I hope you like them!  A lot of them are set in my partner's Flora universe, which you can see more of at Floraverse or on their itch profile.

All of them are cross-platform.  Most of them are free (and open source), but you can support me on Patreon if you would like more of them to exist.

I also run two regular game jams:

GAMES MADE QUICK??? is a low-pressure, week-long jam concurrent with Games Done Quick, intended to spur me into doing something other than watch Twitch for 168 consecutive hours.

  • GAMES MADE QUICK??? (AGDQ 2017)
  • GAMES MADE QUICK??? 1½ (SGDQ 2017)
  • GAMES MADE QUICK??? 2.0 (AGDQ 2018)
  • GAMES MADE QUICK??? 2.5 (SGDQ 2018)
  • GAMES MADE QUICK??? III (AGDQ 2019)

Strawberry Jam runs the entire month of February with a simple goal: make horny games.  It's judged, but the judging isn't very serious and there are no prizes or anything.

  • Strawberry Jam (February 2017)
  • Strawberry Jam 2 (February 2018)
  • Strawberry Jam 3 (February 2019)

I tweet a lot, there's a bunch of other stuff on my website, and I have a low-volume Discord that's mostly used for jam discussion.
