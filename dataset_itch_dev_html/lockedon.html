Hello! I'm LockedOn.

For many years I created content using Scratch, MIT's games engine, where I built a small following. As my content has evolved, I'm moving on to make more ambitious projects with other engines. Itch.io seems like a great platform to release these new projects on.

I've made different types of games, from RPGs (Generation Quest) to mystery games (the Pageturner series, the Kowareta Kokoro series, Methods), to puzzle games (Little Yaga), and I like to try new things and take big risks with my content. Each one of my projects is different, so hopefully there's something for everyone.
