I prefer making games to playing them :) You can find all my games on my website.

I've been making games since I was 7 or 8 years old, and I'm lucky enough to do it for a living today, at Motion Twin. I recently worked on Dead Cells. All my personal creations were done during game jams, mostly Ludum Dare.

I use Haxe language for all my modern stuff, and the Heaps framework.
