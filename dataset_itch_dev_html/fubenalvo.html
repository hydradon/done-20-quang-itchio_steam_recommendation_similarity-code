About me

I'm Zsolt Várady (fubenalvo), an Indie game developer from Budapest, Hungary. I made my first games on GameJams, and Nemesys Games studio gave me a chance to create three games. One of them was Finalist at Indie Prize USA 2016. Later I changed, and my fulltime job is Frontend developer and creating games is a fun hobby for me. 

Since I'm a freelance indie developer, I made two games alone: NeonCode and Flux Caves. And now I joined the team of Chicken Police to create the minigames into this awsome animal/noir! Beside this I'm working on the neoncode sequel: NeonCode 2: Project Moon
