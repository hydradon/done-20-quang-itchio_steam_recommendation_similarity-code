ducklingsmith

ducklingsmith @ducklingsmith

I'm David Mowatt, a solo game creator based in the North West of England.  At the moment I work mostly in Bitsy in the gaps between my day job and running terrible pun-filled games of Dungeons & Dragons.

I made games on old computers when I was a kid and have only really gotten back into it recently, but in the last year my work has appeared at Leftfield @ EGX Rezzed and Feral Vector as part of the Bitsy Boutique, and at the Babycastles gallery in New York, which was not a bad start, really. If you're reading this, I've most recently appeared at AdventureX 2018.

I can be found mostly on my Twitter, and on the Bitsy Discord. Please send me sandwiches.

My current work in progress is the untitled adventures of Skullington, a Bitsy Metroidvania largely inspired by the horrible nightmares I still have about Citadel:
