At Poison Apple Tales, prepare to find delicious stories full of dreams. There are many names for them - visual novels, Choose Your Own Adventures, gamebooks. Essentially, interactive picture books for young adults. They're both a story and a game. Your choices affect the outcome.

Explore a fantasy world. Find romance. Solve a mystery. Get your visual novel games free. Get your otome games free.

We're ready to show you our hopes and dreams, the worlds built inside our heads. We also want to make your dreams come true.

Have you played one of our games? Tell us what you thought about it. What did you think of the characters? Did the story shock you? We want to hear it all! As long as your heart's in the right place, all feedback is welcome.

What you see here are our dreams, but they can be yours, too. So, what are you waiting for? Go on and take a bite.
