Boba Studios

Boba Studios bobastudios.net @bobastudios

Boba Studios is an award-winning, women-owned game developer studio based in Baltimore, Maryland founded by 3 game design students at the Maryland Institute College of Art (MICA) and a composition student from the Peabody Institute. Specializing in digital games, the studio also has experience creating card games, board games, as well as hybrid games. The team of five handles both front- and back-end development of its proprietary software. Boba Studios launched its debut effort, Squirrely Roo Rabbit, in December of 2016. Squirrely Roo Rabbit is an in-development puzzle-platformer game relying on color theory to solve environmental challenges. It features a visual style reminiscent of hand-drawn and -watercolored pop-up storybooks. To date, Boba Studios has created seven games as a team, with its members having individually created 178 games in total.

Boba Studios’ debut game, Squirrely Roo Rabbit, is the first step in Boba Studios’ mission of diversifying and connecting a divided gaming industry. Designed by a team who is primarily women and people of color, Squirrely Roo Rabbit combines what gamers love most about the two largest markets of the gaming industry—indie games' boundless innovation, and AAA games' fully-realized polish—while targeting gaming audiences typically overlooked in the mass market.
