exhausted boi studios @exhaustedbois

exhausted boi studios is run by one exhausted boi. i'm not quite a studio, i'm one person, i just liked the effect of studios. 

i am a 16 year old boy workin' on games just for fun. i am new to game making, with my first game ever being duck goose, my second being overnight, and my third and most recent being duck goose: re-duck. i plan on making many, many more games in the future, and i hope you follow me as i do so.


i would love to assemble a small team of people who are passionate about game making so that exhausted boi studios could pursue bigger and better projects! message me @what.the.kids.call.exhausted on instagram or @exhaustedbois on twitter if you are interested :)

have fun, be safe <3
