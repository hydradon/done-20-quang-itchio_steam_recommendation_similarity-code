Hi there! I'm Whiona, a librarian who makes indie games on the side under the name Opal Starlight. I hope to deliver entertaining story-driven games with memorable characters, fun artwork, and atmospheric environments. I especially love to tell stories with elements of romance, comedy, and fantasy or sci-fi. I also aim to create inclusive games, with a particular focus on LGBTQ positivity!

Check out my devblog for more detailed information about me and my projects, or follow my game dev adventures on Twitter.

My games are 100% free, but if you'd like to send a small donation my way, I'd greatly appreciate it!
