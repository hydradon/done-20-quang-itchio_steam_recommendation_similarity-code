Hey there! I am a portuguese game developer and programmer that works with multiple game engines and different programming languages! My personal game engine of preference is Unity together with C# as it’s scripting language. Depending on the project and time-frame, I can easily adapt and learn other tools.

I am both the co-founder and currently lead programmer of an indie game development team known as Whales And Games through which we develop several mindlessly fun, iterative and full-of-personality games. I am also a game jam aficionado, and often participate in different game jams, most notably Global Game Jam and Ludum Dare. I also volunteer at different industry-related initiatives.

I am also passionate in Game Design and, of course, I also avid into gaming in general.

If you're just looking for a casual chat, you can give me a follow or a mention over at Twitter or if you feel like reading, checkout my development blog!
