Thanks for dropping by! I'm Dana, and/or Sunspider.
Welcome to Gadget Patch, where I grow games for folks.

⚙️ ⚙️ ⚙️

I like to make peaceful, adventurous, playful, and thoughtful games.
I have a penchant for puzzles, unique settings/gameplay, and retro stylings.
I also make free tools, fonts, and open-source art you can use.
I hope my work can inspire you to make things of your own!

🌼 🌼 🌼

My goal is to make free things for everyone, forever, thanks to donations.
If you like something I've made, please consider supporting me.
As a small creator, any support makes a huge difference!
Especially financial support, and sharing my games with your friends

⚙️ ⚙️ ⚙️

Monthly donations on Patreon let me spend time on bigger projects.
Becoming a patron is a good way to see and get involved in my WIPs!
You can also keep me in tea and snacks with one-time Ko-Fi donations.
Twitter is a good place to follow me, and share my work with folks.
And of course, I love getting your comments and feedback.
Knowing people appreciate what I do helps keep me motivated!
