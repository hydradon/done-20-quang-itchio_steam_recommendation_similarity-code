G'day, I'm a games designer and developer in Australia. You might know me from my game Shower With Your Dad Simulator 2015: Do You Still Shower With Your Dad? or my ongoing art project 140 games or less.

I've made all my games (so far!) with Clickteam Fusion 2.5 - if you're new to game design or are interest in game development in any way, I highly recommend checking it out. It's especially handy for rapid prototyping or gamejams.

You can contact me via Twitter, or via this form for technical support.
