Virtually Competent is a small game dev team founded by Izzy Neuhaus and Clover Greenhalgh.

Crystal Control II was our final release and we no longer plan to update any of our titles or develop new ones in the future, but the ones we have are pretty damn good!

Thank you for your support over the years!
