Hi there! I'm Adam "PixelDough" Worrell, and I am an indie game developer. I hope to make lots of games for people to enjoy. I work in PICO-8, Game Maker Studio 2, and Unity. I'm all about being connected with my community, and bringing joy to those around me!

My goal at the end of the day is to provide a means to release the stress that is put on our shoulders by politics, school, work, and life in general.
