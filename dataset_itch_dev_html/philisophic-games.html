Welcome to my page! My name is Philip Johansson, but I usually go with the username ThatSnillet. I live in Sweden and make games as a hobby.

I'm a different kind of gamer and gamedev. I prefer unique experiences that are fun and creative. I try to create all kinds of games in different genres, but only ones that I feel a personal passion for. I don't value tons of playtime or content, but instead giving the player value for their time. I am relatively new to the gamedev scene but I strive to always improve and evolve.

Currently working on the game Hell Punk Horror
