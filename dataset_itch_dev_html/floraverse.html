Hey!  I'm glitchedpuppet, creator of the open world called Flora.  I mostly explore it through a webcomic, Floraverse (and its adults only sister comic, Forbidden Flora), but I've also worked on some Flora-themed games, and here they are!

I sometimes post sneak peeks of games (and comic pages) on my Patreon, and my partner Eevee sets most of her games in Flora as well.
