Rob Colton is a software developer by day, and avid reader of romance novels at night. A romantic at heart, he loves to read and write stories that feature big, burly men who find true love and happy endings.

My current project is a game called Human Cargo. It is a science fiction visual novel featuring bara-inspired hot alien men! It is set in the same universe as my Galactic Conspiracies novels, though it features all new characters and settings.
