Portrait Prophecies www.reddit.com/r/Portr... @noestringfound

Find our community on Reddit at r/PortraitProphecies to keep up to date on news of our most recent games, comics and other creations.

You can also join our Discord server to speak to us directly about out work @ https://discord.gg/9FTwkqr

Portrait Prophecies is a fringe gathering of artists, dedicated to the creation of wholesome art in all its forms. We design games, draw artwork and comics, animate, compose music and write various literature from poetry to novels.

 If you enjoyed any of our work, you can support our cause by rating and commenting on them, or recommending them to your acquaintances. Our greatest struggle lies in growing our audience, anything you can do to spread the word of our works is greatly appreciated. You may also consider donating to us at the download prompt for our games, all our proceeds go towards supporting our operatives in their financial struggles. 

We read every comment we receive across all platforms and appreciate any positive words you may have to express about our work. Unfortunately due to time constraints we typically can only reply to direct questions or practical concerns.

Our static works of art and literature are held on DeviantArt by TheWellCourier and our videos on Youtube by Portrait Prophecies.
