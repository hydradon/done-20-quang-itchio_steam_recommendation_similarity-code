PRYZMAT is a game-making Students’ Club - we’ve been active at the Szczecin Academy of Art since 2017. We created PRYZMAT to share our love of games and make them a part of our Academy’s life. Being a Students’ Club allows us to experiment with them - we try to combine game making with widely understood art. We wish to look for unconventional solutions, new mediums, uncommon mechanics. Through that, we hope to the expand game industry.
