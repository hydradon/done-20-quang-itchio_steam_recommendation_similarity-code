Hi, my name is Noah Burkholder!

I am a game developer and musician, working on some personal projects in what little time I have. I hope you like what I'm making and encourage you to interact with me!

I grew up home-schooled in rural Canada. As a kid I spent a lot of time playing violin, authoring imaginary worlds with my sisters, and playing computer games.
