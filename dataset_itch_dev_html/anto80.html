Hello world!

In my spare time, I'm developing games and stuff for fun or learning purposes.
My preferred engines to do so are Allegro/C++ and Pico-8.

My lab

Some gamejam projects, experimental stuff, or other short projects, most of the time with source code included:


Pico-8 Demo test : Small test I made while playing with the main Pico-8 functions. Rectangles, lines, cosine, random, text with sprites (no fonts).
Music inspired by Space Harrier.
  
Global Game Jam 2016 : In this weird snake game, Player2 controls fruits. It's called 2A2F : Anaconda Awesomeness vs Fruity Freedom. Watch gameplay video or Read more...
  
SpaceBloop : Evolve-Space-Shooter - Destroy boss arms to make them stronger! Pico-8 examples of cartridge save/load data, and direct access to sprite/video memory. Play it in your browser or watch gameplay video.
  
Sausages everywhere : Barbecue time! Can you feed everyone before the sausage fest is over? Play it in your browser.
  
SpinSpin PewPew MewMew 8 : A jamgame I made with 3 other Pico-8 beginners at ADDON 2017: a shoot'em up where some bullets and enemies are hidden depending on their color. Watch gameplay video or download it for free.
  
Forbidden Fortress II : An awful game I made for "Make Something Horrible" gamedev contest, where the goal is to make the worst possible game. Download it for free, watch gameplay video or read the complete list of intended bugs and defects.
