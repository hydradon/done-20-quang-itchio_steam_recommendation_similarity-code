AdventureBox

AdventureBox www.adventurebox.com @AdventureBoxRPG

Adventure Box is a universe of voxel games on the web – made by players, for players. Create your own RPGs and Battlefields – fill them with creatures, traps, and adventures – and share them with friends! Explore the universe with your own character – jumping from world to world via portals, earning loot and leveling up!
