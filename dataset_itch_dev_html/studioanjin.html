Studio Anjin is an independant game development team, working together with big goals in mind!

Bringing together years of shared experience and passion for making games, we've recently made the big dive into working on commercial titles for future release, alongside some freebies along the way!


We regularly stream game development on twitch and one of our team is also a let's player, so check out SwoodDude, lead designer and pixel artist on an upcoming (and soon to be featured on itch.io) title, Woody Chopshrub!

You can also check us out on twitter or get in touch with us through the contact information on our website. We'd be happy to hear from you!
