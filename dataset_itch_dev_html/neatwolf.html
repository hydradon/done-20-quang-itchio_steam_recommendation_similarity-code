Hi!

I am passionate about creating new content and videogames. Videogames

embrace so many fields, and that's why they're my favorite form of art.

Programming/developing videogames has always been my dream since I was a little child. I started by writing on paper my first games, which I could only test on a friend's Commodore 64. Then, I got an Amstrad CPC 464. Then moved to my father's 8086. Then it was the time for the SEGA Genesis and so on.

My style has been heavily influenced by videogames of the 8-bit/16bit era.

Optimization, clever solutions, old school tricks are all in my agenda. I can mix the old with the new seamlessly.

Videogames and videogamers changed a lot these decades and I always try to take the best from each era.
