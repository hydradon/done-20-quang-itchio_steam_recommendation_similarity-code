Raymond Cripps is an independent game designer, character artist and creator of Project Feline.

Raymond is a self-declared autodidact and began developing homemade games, videos and music from his bedroom at the age of eleven. After dropping out of high school Raymond spent several years teaching game design, character animation and programming across a variety of vocational courses in Sydney, Australia.

While teaching, Raymond began developing the protoype for Project Feline using the Unreal Engine, and has been documenting the game's development on YouTube. As of March 2020, his popular Devlog Series has garnered over 1.7 million total views, and his YouTube channel has cultivated an rapidly-growing community of over 49,000 passionate fans and aspiring developers hungry to learn game development!
