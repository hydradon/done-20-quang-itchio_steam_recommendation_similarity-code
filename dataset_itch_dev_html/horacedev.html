Hello, I'm Paul. 

I finally completed my game, Horace and it has released on Steam.

Horace is a pixel platform adventure game that tells an emotional and surprisingly rich tale of self-discovery peppered with nostalgic popular culture references that will bring a smile to any gamer who enjoys the 8 and 16 bit era!

Please play it and share your thoughts.

You can stay up to date by following the Horace Twitter channel.
