Eigen Lenk www.pioneersgame.com @EigenLenk

DOS-enthused game developer from Estonia. I love tinkering with low-res pixelart, especially paired with limited palettes. Have always been a PC guy. Grew up playing DOOM, Duke Nukem 3D & Transport Tycoon (all sorts of management games, really) and other classics in addition to Sierra and LucasArts adventure games.

First game I can recall playing was some sort of Lode Runner-like thing on an old Soviet PC-clone. I was around 5-6, I guess. Later gaming memories include Prince of Persia on an IBM XT with a green tinted display, and later Test Drive 2: The Duel on some 286 with CGA graphics. Appreciation for low-res, low-color kinds of visuals really stems from that.

First started making games in around 2002 with AGI Studio, later moving on to SCI Studio. These can be found on sciprogramming.com

A few years later I started messing with 3D Game Studio and learning C++

These days I work as a full-time developer writing iOS apps here and there.

Thanks for visiting my page and trying out my games!
