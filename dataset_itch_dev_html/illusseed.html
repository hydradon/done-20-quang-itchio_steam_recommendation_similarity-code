Illus Seed

www.illusseed.com @IllusSeed

Illus Seed is a visual novel and game development company created by Cara Hillstock, featuring games developed by her in conjunction with various amazing people. Its first game was Asagao Academy: Normal Boots Club. Other games are currently in the works.

Illus Seed is based on the philosophy of choice. In life, you constantly make choices that impact your future, often in ways you will never realize. We navigate these choices based on emotion, logic, past experience, behavior, projected outcome, and our beliefs and values. Unfortunately, we rarely get a chance to test the consequences of adhering to our values in different situations, and these values can often conflict — should you be honest to your friend, or lie to save his feelings?

Illus Seed aims to examine the illusions we weave throughout our every day reality. Sound interesting? Then check out the games!
