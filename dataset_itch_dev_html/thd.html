Transhuman Design is an independent studio led by Michal Marcinkowski, creator of Soldat, the indie classic that dominated the 2D multiplayer world and directly inspired games like N, Showdown Effect and Take Arms.

Soldat was followed by King Arthur's Gold, a very successful (and crazy) multiplayer build'n'kill game featuring ridable sharks, shields used as parachutes and catapults employed as rapid means of transport into enemy base.

The current projects include Butcher, a blood-soaked love letter to Doom and Quake; Trench Run, a laugh-a-minute platforming shooter; and to the atmospheric, story-driven Transmigration.
