Snozbot

Snozbot fungusgames.com @snozbot

We are an independent games company based in Ireland with 3 founders: John O' Kane, Chris Gregan & Ronan Pearce.

Our games are mostly short, fun, personal projects. We also make the Fungus storytelling plugin for Unity.
