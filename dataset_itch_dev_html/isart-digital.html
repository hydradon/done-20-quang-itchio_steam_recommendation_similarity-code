ISART DIGITAL

ISART Digital is an international school of higher education in video games and 3D-VFX animation. The school is based in Paris & Montreal and has a partnership with the Tokyo University of Technology.

The school is ranked number 1 in Europe and Quebec (Canada) by LinkedIn, on professional integration in the Game and Interactive Media Design market.

The school's reputation has been consolidated by 6 consecutive selections at the Seattle UNITY Awards, and a nomination at THE GAME Awards in Los Angeles.
