About Me

Kinda sorta Game Designer, Programmer, Narrative Designer, Writer, also inclusive event organizer, and nonprofit co-founder, and kind of an entrepreneur? I dunno, I make quirky kinds of games and game like things and stuff. >_>

Currently getting my Master's in Fine Arts in Game Design from NYU's Tisch School of Arts.


Covered by: Rock, Paper, Shotgun, PC Gamer, Weird F***in Games, TechRaptor, Critical Distance, Different Games, Crafting Intangibles, Nerd Nite, Play NYC.

I also founded and organize Unparty at GDC, a quiet event for people to chat in at the Game Developers Conference. I studied Computer Science at Stony Brook University. 

And I'm a Co-Founder and Treasurer of Friendship Garden, a 501c3 nonprofit dedicated to supporting underrepresented game developers.

You can find me hanging around with coolio game devs in New York City.
