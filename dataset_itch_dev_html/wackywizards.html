About the Wacky Wizards

Five game developers with a passion for entertainment

We're five people who enjoy creating games. We started collaborating during a month in Seattle, Washington. Since then we have been developing the couch co-op game EpicShop. 

We believe in creating value and fun for the players. Our faith lies in the creativity and innovation that games and fun can bring to the table. By utilizing this concept, we are able to create interesting and original game concepts that spark fun.
