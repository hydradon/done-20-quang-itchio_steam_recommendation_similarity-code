Hi! I'm RoAnna Sylver, author of CHAMELEON MOON, STAKE SAUCE, and other cool, weird, diverse fiction. All of which are up here, or will be soon! (RUNTIME and ALWAYS BE YOU are free and always will be!)

I've been writing books for a long time, drawing things for them almost as long, and now interactive fiction (Twine, and also Choicescript, because Choice of Games is publishing my big-ass portal fantasy, DAWNFALL!) so I have a whole new way to be a queer nerd.

Thanks so much for checking out my stuff - Itch.io gives creators MUCH more money than Other Websites I Could Name, so I greatly appreciate you being here!
