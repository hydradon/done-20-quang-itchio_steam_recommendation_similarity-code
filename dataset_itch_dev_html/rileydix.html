RileyDix

My name is Riley Dix, I'm a growing game designer and writer in SoCal, and this is my Portfolio page on Itch.io. My games stand apart from others because my design philosophy is influenced by writing; I want the player to experience a sort of storytelling while they play, even if that story isn't necessarily the plot of the game. I make games that build their own narratives every time they are played.

The games here were all made by myself alone, or myself with a partner, over the course of a few weeks each.

I have recently worked with UCSC's Game Design Association on the game Seeds of Love, on the design and writing teams, and was responsible for UI design and editing portions of the visual novel.

In addition, over the past summer I worked as a design intern at the game company Mythical Games, and worked in the capacity as a junior designer on their upcoming game: Blankos Block Party.
