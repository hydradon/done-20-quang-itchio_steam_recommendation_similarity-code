Born Frustrated is a small group of anarchic-syndicalist developers, or otherwise called, an indie team.

Our collaborators craft these tasty games in the clouds, working from home with their trusted machines and a lot of passion.

Indeed, there isn't such thing like a "main" or "official" team, we desire to create unique experimental games with the craziest and talented people out there.
Do you like our style? - then, come forward.
