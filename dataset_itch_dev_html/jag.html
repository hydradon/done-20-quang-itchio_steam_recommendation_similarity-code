ABOUT JENNIFER ANN'S GROUP

Jennifer Ann's Group is a nonprofit charity preventing abuse. We believe in the power of video games to create positive change in the world and since 2008 have been producing serious games to engage, educate, and empower. Our Gaming Against Violence program is an award-winning and research-based approach to preventing abuse.


GASLIGHTING GAMES

The 2019 Life.Love. Game Design Challenge theme was: GASLIGHTING. The winning games are:

  • 'Last Shred of Reality' from Another Kind, Belgium
  • 'Lamplight Hollow' from Luciano Sgarbi, UK
  • 'LED Gaslight' from Naomi Norbez, USA
  • 'Gaslighting Under the Sea' from Sam Schneider, USA

The games will be published soon. Follow us on social media or on itch.io to be notified when these gaslighting games are released.

BYSTANDER GAMES

The 2018 Life.Love. Game Design Challenge theme was: BYSTANDERS. The contest ran from April 6, 2018 - May 6, 2018 and these games are now available in beta: Citizen Witch, Step Up.

CONSENT GAMES

The Consent Game Series includes a variety of serious games intended to teach young and old about consent. The games that have been published in this series are: ADRIFT, Crossing Boundaries, How to Blorrble-Blobble, and Stuck in a Dark Place. All of the games in the Consent Game Series are free for educators, students, and parents but not all of the games are appropriate for all ages.

NEW! Rispek Danis is a video game developed collaboratively between World Vision Vanuatu and Jennifer Ann's Group to produce a culturally relevant video game intended to teach young people in Vanuatu about the meaning and importance of consent. Note: Rispek Danis is in Bislama; the Respect Dance, an English version of the Respect Dance is available here to play in your browser.

TEEN DATING VIOLENCE GAMES

Jennifer Ann's Group has produced dozens of video games to help young people recognize the warning signs of an abusive relationship. Some of the games include Grace's Diary and little things. More games about healthy relationships are available at Jennifer Ann's Group's game portal.
