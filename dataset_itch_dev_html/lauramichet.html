Laura Michet

I'm a game developer and writer working in Los Angeles. I make my own personal projects, and also do contract work for other developers. (I wrote for Frog Fractions 2 , Where the Water Tastes Like Wine, and Industries of Titan, and did localization writing for Pathologic 2.) Most of my personal projects worth playing can be found here on itch.

If you're interested in talking to me about the source files for my Twine projects or anything else, reach out on twitter!
