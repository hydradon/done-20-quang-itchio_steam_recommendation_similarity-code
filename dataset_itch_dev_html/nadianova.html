nadia nova nadianova.fi @littanana

i’m a finnish artist who draws, makes games and writes stories on my free time. most of my content focuses on mirroring my own experiences— being a trans woman navigating sex, being gay and mentally ill.

i aim to create stories and art with heavy focus on trans positivity, cute and fluffy sexual stories, trans people being unapologetically trans, casual furry art and sometimes just straight up porn.

sometimes my work touches up on more heavy themes, dealing with mental illness, abandonment issues, just trying to manage daily life and other various problems, i either face or have faced in my life.

if you like what i do, consider, supporting my patreon <3

if you want access to some of my paid content but don't have the extra, just email me and i can probably just give it for you!  it's all here to give me a bit income for my work, but i don't feel happy locking a fan away from my content! nadiaprogynova@gmail.com

my main goal in life is to fill itch.io's #transgender tag with games
