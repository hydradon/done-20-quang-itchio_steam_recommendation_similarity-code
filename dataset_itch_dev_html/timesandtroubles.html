Sav Emmett Wolfe

@TimesNTroubles

Sav Emmett Wolfe is a triple award winning games writer and producer. His standout game, That Boy is A Monstr has tugged on the heart strings internationally for his honest and open portrayal of the fears and misconceptions of dating while transgender.

His work focuses on two key aspects: Representation and education. These aspects move through all he does, from his management style to his writing.


Trans man, Queer. He/Him.
