Hi, I'm Freya C! I'm a UK-based indie games dev who makes narrative games that are usually:

  • science fiction, horror, &/or romance
  • close to 100% LGBTQ characters
  • free/PWYW with low tech requirements
  • liable to make players keysmash due to feelings

If you like my work, you can support me to make more on patreon: patreon.com/spdrcstl
