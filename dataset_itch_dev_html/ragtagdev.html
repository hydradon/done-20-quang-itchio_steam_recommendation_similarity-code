Ragtag Developments Ltd

www.ragtagdev.com @RagtagDev

Ragtag Developments Ltd is an independent, award-winning, interactive software development studio based in Oxfordshire, UK, specialising in high-end interactives, apps, games and the use of video game technology in other business sectors.

In 2012 Ragtag were proud to win an innovation award for an in-development internal project which is going live later this year. The core team consists of 4 professional developers with a combined games industry experience of over 60 years. Their expertise covers all major mobile, desktop, server, web and console technologies, using an in-house custom graphics engine.
