Hello there !

I'm primarily an illustrator and a 2D artist by trade in Malaysia, but I love games, unhealthily a lot. I love reading about the lore and stories, playing with the mechanics, learning the development histories, and generally all round having fun. Inevitably, I do make a couple here and there occasionally ~

Below are the ones I've uploaded to itch.io, there are a few older ones on my site but these are the more recent ones. Gasbound is relatively long, but the rest are all quite short and can be enjoyed in about 3 to 5 minutes each.
