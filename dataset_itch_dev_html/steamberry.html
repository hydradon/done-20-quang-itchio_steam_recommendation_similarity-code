Steamberry Studio is an Australia-based indie production team with a love for narrative driven games. Our current focus is creating fun visual novels with multi-layered stories, richly developed characters, and pretty art.  

Our debut project is Changeling - a supernatural urban fantasy about magical people and Faeries. Changeling released in January 2019.

Our current project is Gilded Shadows - a science fiction adventure game with a heavy splash of romance. Gilded Shadows is expected to Kickstart in February 2020.

Please check out our tumblr blog for weekly updates about our current project, Gilded Shadows, and its development and progress!
