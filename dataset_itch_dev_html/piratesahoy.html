PiratesAhoy! is a community for all fans of pirates and the Age of Sail, centred around games, books, and movies.

We are currently developing New Horizons Remastered, our own non-commercial indie title and spiritual successor to Hearts of Oak. 

We have traditionally been a modding community, our flagship works being the unified Build Mod (AKA New Horizons) for Akella's 2003 game Pirates of the Caribbean, and the Gentlemen of Fortune mod for their more recent game, Age of Pirates 2: City of Abandoned Ships.

The community is free for anyone to join, regardless of age, nationality or ability, and we always warmly welcome any and all new members to our site. We're always on the lookout for beta testers, coders, modellers, texture artists and anyone with new ideas! For more information and to join the community, please visit our main forum: www.piratesahoy.net.
