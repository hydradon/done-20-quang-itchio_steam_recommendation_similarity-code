Matt Boyd-Surka is an independent sound designer, game developer, and multimedia artist based in Philadelphia, PA.

Although Matt usually works alone, he is sometimes joined by friends and family for specific projects. The moniker used to describe this quasi-studio is getinthedamnbox.
