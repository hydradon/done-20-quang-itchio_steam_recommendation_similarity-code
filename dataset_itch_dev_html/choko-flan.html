About Me:

Hi there, I'm Choko!

I'm a indie developer who has a passion for story driven games, character design, and world building. I've been developing games since 2013, but finished my first game in 2016. I'm a big fan of mixing cute aesthetic with psychological horror. Though I do also enjoy making light hearted game as well.
