I make digital things and Brain Blinks is my virtual studio.

I'm a digital tinkerer living in the Willamette Valley of Oregon, USA. I've been making things with computers for 35 years using every creative tool I can get my hands on. For the last 5 years I've been using Unity to combine my various digital design skills into experimental projects, arcade games, and VR experiences.

You can see more of my digital doodles over at BrainBlinks.com or follow me on Google+ or Twitter.
