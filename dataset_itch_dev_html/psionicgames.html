Hi, my name is Si, although most people online know me as 'Psionic', I'm from the UK and I've been doing 2D/3D Art and Game Design as a one man team for over 10 years now and have a website at www.psionicgames.com

I mostly create point & click adventure games with a horror or Sci-Fi edge but I also dabble in all kinds of crazy game ideas in between!

I have been working as a freelance 2D/3D Game artist for around 10 years at this point and I've been creating games using FLASH for various portals…and I've also worked on some pretty high profile advergame projects for Addicting Games like Star Trek, Iron Man 2, TinTin, MIB 3, Kung Fu Panda 2, Henry Headshot, BMX Baboon to name a few!!

I am probably most well known for creating the Being One series, Killer Escape series, Ghostscape 1, 2 & 3D, Urbex, Icescape, Tombscape etc - basically for creating loads of flash point & click escape adventure games ;-)

I have recently started using UNITY to create webGL, HTML 5 and Android games, so check out my games page for my latest stuff!!
