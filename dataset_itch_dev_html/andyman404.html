Hi, my name is Andrew C. Wang. I am an indie game developer, living in Honolulu, Hawaii.

I create experimental video games and virtual reality experiences. Most of my games were created during game jams with weird themes. I've done 100+ game jams in the past few years.

Some of my games are here on Itch, some are on Gamejolt, and some are scattered on various other sites. But you can find most of them on my portfolio page:
